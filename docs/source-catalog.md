## Catalog structure

- 300 nationwide questions
- 10 questions for each of Germany's 16 federal states
- 460 entries in the full BAMF PDF
- 310 relevant questions for one learner: the 300 nationwide questions plus the 10 questions for their federal state

The real exam sheet contains 33 questions: 30 nationwide questions and 3 questions for the learner's federal state. BAMF currently states that the `Leben in Deutschland` test is passed with at least 15 correct answers; at least 17 correct answers are needed when using it as proof of knowledge for naturalization.

## Working files

`../data/catalog_raw_text.txt` is a searchable text extraction from the official PDF. It is not yet application-ready data. The official catalog includes image-based answers (for example flags, maps, and coats of arms), so the PDF remains the source of truth until those assets and answer choices are extracted and verified.
